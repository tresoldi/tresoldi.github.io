## Research

Computational historical linguistics: **phylogenetics** of languages and text traditions;
sound correspondences and the inference of **sound change**; native **South American
languages** (Tupí-Guaraní, Tupían, Arawan) and the **Uralic** family; **lexical databases**
(Arca Verborum, GLED) and the CLDF ecosystem. See [research](/research/) for detail.

## Software

Author and maintainer of open-source tools for computational linguistics — among them
`arcaverborum`, `gled`, `ngesh`, `maniphono`, `alteruphono`, `malign`, `dafsa`, and
`nhandu`. Full catalogue on the [software](/software/) page and on
[GitHub](https://github.com/tresoldi).
